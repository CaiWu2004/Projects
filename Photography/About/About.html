This project is about the history of Chinatown in New York City, one of the most iconic and oldest Chinatowns in the United States. It covers the history of Chinatown, from its construction to its present day, as well as some of the most iconic places in Chinatown, such as Nom Wah Tea Parlor and McDonald's on Doyers Street. The project also includes a gallery of photos taken by me during my visit to Chinatown, as well as a reference page with all the sources I used for this project.
